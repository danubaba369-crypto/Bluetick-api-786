Facebook Lead Generation

Overview

The Facebook Lead Integration module in Wapi enables you to connect your Facebook Lead Ads directly to your WhatsApp automation workflows. This ensures that every lead captured on Facebook is immediately synchronized into your Wapi contact list, allowing for instant automated engagement.

  • Instant Sync: Leads from Facebook Instant Forms are synced in real-time.
  • Automated Outreach: Trigger immediate WhatsApp welcome messages using pre-approved templates.
  • Smart Mapping: Map Facebook form fields to custom Wapi contact attributes.
  • Lead Categorization: Automatically assign tags to leads based on the form they submitted.

Connecting Instant Forms

To start capturing leads, you must first link your existing Facebook Lead Gen forms to the platform.

  • Navigate to Marketing > Lead Generation Forms.
  • Switch to the Instant Forms tab to view all forms available on your connected Facebook Pages.
  • Identify the form you wish to use and click the Link (Chain) icon under the Actions column.
  • Once linked, the form will move to the Connected Forms tab and start receiving real-time updates via webhooks.

Form Mapping & Configuration

Proper field mapping ensures that data collected from Facebook (like email or phone number) is stored in the correct fields within your Wapi contact database.

Field Mapping

Map each Facebook field to its corresponding Wapi Contact Field using the dropdown menus. For example, map full_name to Contact Name.

Lead Badges & Tags

Use the Auto-assign Tags feature to automatically label incoming leads. This helps in segmenting your audience for future broadcast campaigns.

First Message Automation

Maximize your conversion rate by sending an instant WhatsApp message to your new leads the moment they submit a form.

  • Toggle Enable: Use the switch to activate automated welcome messages for the specific form.
  • Select Template: Choose an approved WhatsApp template (Marketing, Utility, etc.) to be sent.
  • Variable Mapping: Map dynamic placeholders in your template (e.g., {{1}}) to Facebook fields (e.g., full_name) for a personalized greeting.

Managing Connected Forms

The Connected Forms tab provides an overview of all active lead capture funnels.

  • Webhook Status: Ensure the status shows Subscribed to receive leads.
  • Actions: Use the Table icon to revisit mapping settings or the Delete icon to stop syncing a specific form.
  • Refresh: Click the refresh button to synchronize the latest form data from Meta servers.

Best Practices

  • Required Fields: Always ensure the phone_number field is correctly mapped, as it is required for WhatsApp automation.
  • Template Content: Use engaging templates that clearly identify your brand and reference the Facebook ad the user clicked.
  • Testing: Use the Facebook Lead Ads Testing Tool to verify that leads are flowing into Wapi correctly before launching a large campaign.

Important: If you change the fields in your Facebook form, remember to update the mapping in Wapi to prevent data loss or automation failures.
