Billing & Payments

To send business-initiated messages (e.g., Marketing, Utility, or Authentication templates) via the WhatsApp Cloud API across the WAPI platform, you must configure a valid payment method in your Meta Business Manager.

1

1. Overview

  • Meta uses a postpaid billing model.
  • Charges are applied based on:
    • Template messages
    • Pricing categories (Marketing, Utility, Authentication)
  • Billing is automatically deducted from the added payment method.
2

2. Before You Begin (IMPORTANT)

  • You must complete WhatsApp setup in Meta Developer Console.
  • Go to: WhatsApp → Configuration → Manage phone numbers.

This redirects to your business portfolio. Only after this step, you can add a payment method.

Note

If you haven't completed setup in the developer console, you will NOT be able to add a payment method.

3

3. Required Permission

You must have:

  • Manage WhatsApp account permission

Note

If you are the owner of the WhatsApp Business Account, you already have this permission.

4

4. Add Credit Card (Step-by-Step)

Instructions:

  1. Open WhatsApp Manager.
  2. Go to Overview page.
  3. Select your account.
  4. Click the 3-dot menu.
  5. Click Manage account settings.
  6. Open Settings tab.
  7. Click Payment settings.
business.facebook.com/wa/manager/settings

Billing & Payments:

  • In Billing & Payments, click Add payment method.
  • Follow instructions:
    • Add payment details.
    • Click Next.
business.facebook.com/billing_hub
business.facebook.com/billing_hub/add
  • Enter card details → Click Save.
  • Add business information → Click Save.
  • Go to account
  • The billing section displays the total outstanding balance associated with your account.
business.facebook.com/wa/manager/overview
5

5. After Adding Payment Method

  • Credit card will be linked to your WhatsApp Business Account.
  • You can view it in: Settings → Payment settings.
6

6. How Charges Are Applied

  • Charges are NOT applied immediately.
  • Billing is based on:
    • Template message usage
    • Pricing category

Note

Charges will be calculated as per Meta pricing (Marketing, Utility, Authentication categories).

7

7. Important Restriction

Without adding a payment method, you cannot:

  • Send template messages.
  • Start business-initiated conversations.
  • Use marketing, utility, or authentication messages.

Your account may remain:

  • Restricted
  • In test mode
8

8. Common Issues

  • Payment option not visible: Setup not completed in Developer Console.
  • Permission missing: You do not have "Manage WhatsApp account" access.
  • Payment failed: Your outgoing messages may stop immediately.
9

9. Best Practices

  • Add payment method before going live.
  • Keep backup payment method.
  • Monitor billing usage regularly.
  • Verify business account.
